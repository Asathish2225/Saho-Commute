What Founders are Excited About

Hear from developers and riders embracing innovation.

"Back in my college days, the only option to get in and out of campus was the shuttle bus service. Most of the time, it was overcrowded, leading to delays and frustrations. On top of that, during emergencies, it was almost impossible to get in or out of campus quickly. Other alternatives like bikes or autos were far too expensive for a regular commute. That's when I realized there had to be a better, more efficient way to travel. So, I came up with the idea of an easy, accessible bike-sharing solution — something that lets you take a bike anytime you need it, travel efficiently, and avoid the hassles of rush hours and high costs. It’s designed with one goal: to make your commute simple, stress-free, and affordable."

Sathish, Co-founder at Saha Commute

"This app has revolutionized how I commute daily. With its intuitive design and seamless connectivity to the nearest bike stations, I find and unlock bikes in seconds. The whole process from registration, unlocking, and payment is streamlined, leaving me more time to focus on what really matters — exploring the college surrounding without hassle or delay. Highly recommend it!"

Sathish, Co-founder at Saha Commute
